The Inital Models

Inorder to gauge the effectivness of adding in sentiment analysis, we first trained two models on only the collected finacial data. Initally we planed on only using one model, but out of concern for algorithmic bias, we decided to use two. The first model is a gradient boosted tree from the lightgbm library, it was the better preformer of the two with a mean squared error of .000185. The second is a neural network from the sklearn library, it had a mean squared error of .000418

The black dashed line shows the split between the training/validation data and the test data

As you can see, while both networks match major trends in the data, they are not very accurate. This is alright for our purposes as we are not trying to make the most accurate model, but rather test the accuacy of adding sentiment analysis into our data. Let's take a look below!
